FlowerPhobia

C#, Unity URP, GameJam, Teamwork

In the game you take role of a child who is afraid of flowers and need to destroy a grandmas garden while escaping her grasp and dodging stones. Destroy continously spawning flowers to lower your anxiety, use gnomes as grenades against flowers and aim to reach high score.

A first-person game prototype delivered by a three-person team within a strict 72-hour game jam.
I was responsible for implementing key gameplay mechanics and systems, including the throwing gnome mechanic (used for AOE flower killing and associated pickup logic), the stone stun effect on the player, and the flower destruction system. This project delivered crucial experience in fast coding under pressure, collaborating effectively via Git to meet the tight 72-hour deadline.
